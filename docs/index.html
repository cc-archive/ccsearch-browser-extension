CC Search Extension

A cross-browser extension, which lets you search for and filter content that is under Creative Commons licenses.



Use on your favorite browser

Chrome Firefox Opera

Features

SEARCH AND FILTER CC-LICENSED CONTENT


Search through millions of CC-licensed images and use filters to find the content that best meets your needs.



ONE-CLICK DOWNLOAD WITH EASY ATTRIBUTION


Crediting the creator of CC-licensed content found using the extension is easy with one-click attribution.



BOOKMARK YOUR FAVORITE IMAGES


Bookmarking images will save them in the extension. You can view and remove your bookmarks from the Bookmarks section.



Dark Mode


Dark Mode! Because everyone loves it. Good for your eyes and saves battery.


More features for more productivity


Export and import bookmarks
User-interface (UI) options available for custom settings
Sync your custom settings and bookmarks across devices
