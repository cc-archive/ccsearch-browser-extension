CC Search Extension

A Cross-Browser Extension which lets you search and filter content in the public domain and under Creative Commons licenses.



Use on your favourite browser

Chrome Firefox Opera

Features

Search and filter CC Licensed content


Search through million of images and filter based on source website, types of licenses, and/or use-case.



One-Click download with easy attribution


Download images with rich-text or HTML version attribution in just one-click.



Bookmark your favourite Images


Save images for future use, just hover and click on bookmark icon to bookmark images.



Dark Mode


Dark Mode! Because everyone loves it. Good for your eyes and saves battery.


More features for more productivity


Export and import bookmarks
User-interface (UI) options available for custom settings
Sync your custom settings and bookmarks across devices
