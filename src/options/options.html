What is the sync feature of the extension?

Chrome and Firefox have a built-in feature that syncs browser settings and preferences across your logged-in devices. The extension leverages this feature to sync your custom settings and bookmarks. This guarantees that the content you have bookmarked on your work computer will be present on your personal computer.

How do I activate the sync feature in Google Chrome?
  • Open the Chrome browser.
  • Paste "chrome://settings/syncSetup" in the address bar and press enter.
  • Turn on sync by pressing the "Turn on" button.
  • Click on "Manage sync".
  • Toggle on the "Extensions" option if not already turned on.
How do I activate the sync feature in Mozilla Firefox?
  • Open the Firefox browser.
  • Click the Firefox Account icon in the toolbar.
  • Click "Sign in to Firefox".
  • After signing in, click on the Firefox Account icon again and click on "Sync Settings".
  • You will see the items you are syncing. If "Add-ons" is not present in the list, click on the "Change" button and select it.
How do I update the extension?

We constantly push new versions of the extension with bug fixes and new features. By default, the extension is updated to the new version automatically by your browser, but this may not happen immediately. To update the extension manually you can perform the following steps:

Google Chrome

  • Open the Chrome browser.
  • In the address bar, type "chrome://extensions" and press enter.
  • At the top of the extensions page, toggle on the "Developer mode" option.
  • Press the "Update" button

Mozilla Firefox

  • Open the Firefox browser.
  • In the address bar, type "about:addons" and press enter.
  • Click the gear icon in the upper-right area of the Extensions panel.
  • Click the "Check for updates" option.

Opera

  • Open the Opera browser.
  • Click on menu > Extensions > Extensions (or press Ctrl + Shift + E)
  • At the top of the extensions page, toggle on the "Developers mode" option.
  • Press the "Update" button.
